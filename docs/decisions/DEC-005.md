# DEC-005: Signal Layer Weights (4-Layer Stack)

**Decision Date:** 10 May 2026  
**Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

---

## CONTEXT

7-layer architecture requires weighting 4 active layers (L1-L3, L6):
1. **Layer 1 (Tech):** Momentum, technicals, market structure
2. **Layer 2 (Macro):** USD/TRY, CDS, Brent, yield curve
3. **Layer 3 (KAP):** Corporate news, ownership changes
4. **Layer 6 (Risk):** Kelly Criterion, drawdown limits

**Question:** How to combine these signals into single portfolio score?

---

## DECISION

Fixed weights determined by empirical tuning (backtested 2024-2026):

| Layer | Name | Weight | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|---|
| **L1** | Tech | 20% | Timing/momentum (important but secondary) |
| **L2** | Macro | 33% | BIST highly sensitive to macro regime (primary) |
| **L3** | KAP | 27% | Corporate signals capture edge cases |
| **L6** | Risk | 20% | Position sizing (to be implemented) |

---

## FORMULA

```
Portfolio_Score = (L1 × 0.20 + L2 × 0.33 + L3 × 0.27 + L6 × 0.20) / 0.80

When fully implemented:
= (L1 × 0.20 + L2 × 0.33 + L3 × 0.27 + L4 × w4 + L5 × w5 + L6 × 0.20) / 1.0
```
